Mace Slam

INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to Mace Slam! Defend the castle from intruders with your powerful mace!

  • Use W,A,S, and D to move your character
  • Use the arrow keys to move the mace

You can press the "R" key to return to the main menu at any time. (You will lose your progress.)

Clear all enemies to win the game.

If your character collides with an enemy, you'll lose health. If enemies reach the bottom of the screen, your castle will be damaged.
If your health or castle reaches zero, you lose.

The mace has a limited range. When your chain begins to flash red, you're trying to move the mace outside of range. Try moving your character closer!

The mace is designed to be somewhat difficult to get used to. If you need a safe area to learn the mace's physics, try Training mode!

Reference for Main Menu

  • Pink Square = Classic mode
  • Red Square = Hard mode
  • Purple Square = Insane mode
  • Orange Rectangle = Training mode
